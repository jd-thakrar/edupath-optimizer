Every year, thousands of students fail courses not because they lack ability, but because risks aren't identified early enough. Traditional academic systems are reactive - they intervene only after students have already failed.

What if AI could predict academic risk before it becomes a problem?

Meet EduPath Optimizer - an AI-powered early warning system that predicts student failure with 96% accuracy and provides personalized interventions.

EduPath Optimizer combines four powerful AI technologies:

First - A Gradient Boosting machine learning model trained on attendance patterns, performance trends, and engagement metrics.

Second - Explainable AI using Google's Gemini API that translates complex predictions into plain English.

Third - A knowledge graph that maps course prerequisites and propagates risk across the curriculum.

And fourth - A counterfactual engine that simulates "what-if" scenarios to recommend the most effective interventions.

Let me show you how it works.

Students log in using Firebase authentication with email or Google Sign-In.

The dashboard immediately shows their risk assessment. Here we see a student with a 38% failure probability - that's medium risk.

The AI explains WHY: declining attendance over 12 weeks, performance volatility in Data Structures, and engagement metrics below threshold. These aren't just numbers - they're actionable insights.

Real-time charts visualize attendance trends and performance across multiple subjects. Notice how the system tracks temporal patterns, not just single snapshots.

Here's where it gets powerful. The AI Insights page shows three evidence-based interventions ranked by effectiveness.

"Improve Attendance to 90%" reduces risk by 12% with moderate effort. "Boost Test Performance by 5 marks" cuts risk by 10%. Each intervention shows expected outcomes with AI-generated explanations.

Students can even simulate scenarios in real-time: "What if I improve my attendance AND my test scores?" The system recalculates instantly, showing a predicted risk drop to 18%.

Notice the same student ID appears across both pages - that's our data consistency feature ensuring coherent analysis throughout the platform.

For administrators, the admin dashboard provides system-wide oversight. We're analyzing 100 students with 20% flagged as high risk.

Visual analytics break down risk distribution. The high-risk student table prioritizes intervention targets - students with 90% or higher failure probability need immediate attention.

Behind the scenes, the knowledge graph works continuously. It identifies that struggling in Database Systems puts students at 45% risk in Advanced Algorithms next semester - enabling proactive curriculum planning and prerequisite support.

The technical foundation is robust: Flask backend with 15 REST APIs, Firebase Firestore for real-time data synchronization, and Chart.js for interactive visualizations. The machine learning model achieves 96% accuracy using 12 engineered temporal features extracted from student behavior patterns.

The entire system is production-ready. Over 10,000 lines of code, fully deployed on Render.com with continuous integration and deployment. Comprehensive documentation covers everything from API endpoints to deployment guides.

But EduPath Optimizer isn't just about technology - it's about impact.

Traditional systems wait for students to fail before offering help. We identify risks weeks or months in advance. We don't just predict failure - we provide clear, personalized pathways to success.

Every intervention is evidence-based, ranked by effectiveness, and explained in plain language. Students understand not just WHAT to do, but WHY it works and HOW much it will help.

This is education transformed from reactive to proactive. From generic to personalized. From opaque algorithms to transparent AI that empowers students to take control of their academic journey.

Because every student deserves more than a failing grade. They deserve a warning. A plan. And a chance to succeed.

That's EduPath Optimizer.
